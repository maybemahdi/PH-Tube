Understanding JavaScript: Scopes and REST

Posted on August 24, 2023

JavaScript is a versatile and powerful language, but it can also be tricky to master. In this article, we'll explore some of the fundamental concepts of JavaScript, including variable scope, null and undefined, and the basics of working with REST APIs.

Scope of var, let, and const

Understanding variable scope is crucial to writing clean and maintainable JavaScript code. In this section, we'll take a closer look at the differences between var, let, and const, and how they affect the scope of variables in JavaScript.

Use cases of null and undefined

In JavaScript, null and undefined are used to represent the absence of a value. While they may seem similar at first glance, they have different use cases and behaviors. In this section, we'll explore when to use null and undefined, and how to handle them properly in your code.

What is REST API?

The term "REST API" is often used in the context of web development, but what does it actually mean? In this section, we'll explain the principles of REST, the key concepts of RESTful APIs, and how to make requests to a REST API using JavaScript.
